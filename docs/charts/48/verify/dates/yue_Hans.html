Key to Escaped Characters

❰TSP❱Thin spaceA space character that is narrower (in most fonts) than the regular one.
❰NBTSP❱No-break thin spaceSame as thin space, but doesn’t line break.
❰NDASH❱En dashSlightly wider (–) than a hyphen (-), used for ranges of numbers or dates in some languages.
